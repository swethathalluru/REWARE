### 📐 **Photo Requirements**

**Dimensions:** 300px width × 400px height (3:4 aspect ratio)
**Format:** JPG or PNG
**Size:** Keep under 500KB for fast loading
**Quality:** High resolution, clear and well-lit

### 📷 **Photo Tips**

1. **Good Lighting:** Use natural light or bright, even lighting
2. **Clean Background:** White or neutral background works best
3. **Full Item View:** Show the complete clothing item
4. **Multiple Angles:** Consider front view as primary
5. **Professional Look:** Iron/steam clothes before photographing

#### **Option 1: Take Your Own Photos**

- Follow the photo tips above
