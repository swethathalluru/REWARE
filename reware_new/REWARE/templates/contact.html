Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure quality?

Every item goes through a rigorous inspection process. We check for authenticity, condition, and quality before listing any product on our platform.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange.

How fast is shipping?

We offer free standard shipping (5-7 business days) and express shipping (2-3 business days) options. All orders are processed within 24 hours.

Can I sell my clothes to Reware?

Yes! We're always looking for quality pre-loved clothing. Contact us to learn about our consignment program and selling process.
