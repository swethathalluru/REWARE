Our Mission

At Reware, we're on a mission to revolutionize the fashion industry by making sustainable clothing accessible to everyone. We believe that great fashion shouldn't come at the cost of our planet.

Every year, millions of tons of clothing end up in landfills. We're changing that by giving quality garments a second life, reducing waste, and creating a circular economy where fashion is both stylish and sustainable.
